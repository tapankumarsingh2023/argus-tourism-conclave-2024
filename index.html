Tourism Conclave Shoots

ARGUS CHALO DEKHEIN APNA DESHSEASON 3

A one-day Conclave scheduled for 9 March 2024 [Saturday] to unlock India's rich tapestry of tourist destination. It will serve as a dynamic platform for promoting the country's diverse cultural, historical, and natural treasures to the global audience. The intent is to foster collaboration, embrace technology, and showcase the nation's cultural wealth. It will set the stage for a new era of global tourism centered around India's unique and enchanting offerings.

The participants of the Conclave will include key stakeholders from the tourism industry, government officials [policymakers], and international influencers.
